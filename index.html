Ismail Lotfi

Postdoctoral Fellow, Khalifa University, UAE

Bibliography

Ismail Lotfi received his Ph.D. degree in 2023 from the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. His main research interests include the area of resource management, generative-AI, game theory and incentive mechanism design in communication networks.

Education

  • PhD, Computer Science & Engengineering, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, 2023
  • Master, Computer Networks & Security, Oran 1 University, Algeria, 2017
  • Bachelor, Computer Science, USTO-MB, Algeria, 2015

Employment History

  • Postdoctoral Fellow, Khalifa University, UAE, 2024 - present
  • Postdoctoral Fellow (remote), HBKU, Qatar, 2023 - 2024
